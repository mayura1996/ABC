QUICK MED

Patients have to buy medications from pharmacy other than the hospitals in most of the time. However, patients won't be able to purchase all the medication in a single place. This wastes time and and causes stress and anxiety on patient. To over come these inconviniences, a system that selects the pharmacy which can provide most of the prescribed medication with ready-to-pick service is required.QuickMed is the solution for the above problems.QuickMed is a mobile application along with a web-based portal.Quickmed can select the nearest pharmacy which can provide most of the prescribed medication,Make the medicine pack ready at the pharmacy by the time of arrival, Notifying the time to have the medicines in advance,Saves the time to search for medicines
